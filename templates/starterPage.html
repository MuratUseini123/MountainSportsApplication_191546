About us

“Mountain Sports Application” Is an application which will be used by athletes, for all running and cycling enthusiasts but also for people who practice hiking and many other sports. This application is also for people which has some disabilities which limit their physical function (hearing, visual, mobility etc.). This application will have many functionalities including listing all the possible routes for your trip, finding routes by searching which is crucial functionality because not every user knows every route which they are going to walk. “Mountain Sports Application” will be commonly used for outdoor activities such as hiking, mountain biking, climbing and many other activities.
